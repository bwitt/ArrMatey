ArrMatey is an all-in-one mobile companion for your home server media stack. Support for Sonarr, Radarr, Lidarr, Bookshelf, Listenarr, Prowlarr, and Seerr is included with many more coming!

<b>Combined Dashboard</b>
<li>
<ul>Centralized overview of all instances, download clients, and requests</ul>
<ul>Customizable layout: add, remove, and reorder cards to fit your workflow</ul>
<ul>Real-time network status and detailed disk space monitoring</ul>
<ul>Quick-access shortcuts to your recently added media and upcoming schedule</ul>
</li>

<b>Multi-Instance Support</b>
<li>
<ul>Manage multiple Sonarr, Radarr, Lidarr, Bookshelf, and Listenarr instances</ul>
<ul>Seamless integration with Prowlarr for indexer management and Seerr for requests</ul>
<ul>Quick instance switching and custom instance labels</ul>
</li>

<b>Library Management</b>
<li>
<ul>Browse your entire media library with list and grid views</ul>
<ul>Search and filter by title, quality, and monitored status</ul>
<ul>Sort by name, date added, size, and more</ul>
<ul>View detailed media information, including episode lists, season management, and file status</ul>
<ul>Dedicated management for Audiobooks with metadata and file tracking</ul>
</li>

<b>Interactive Search</b>
<li>
<ul>Manual search for releases via Arr instances or Prowlarr</ul>
<ul>Filter by quality, language, indexer, and protocol</ul>
<ul>View seeders, size, age, and custom format scores</ul>
<ul>One-tap download with confirmation for rejected releases</ul>
</li>

<b>Calendar View</b>
<li>
<ul>View upcoming episodes, movies, albums, and books</ul>
<ul>Switch between list and month views</ul>
<ul>Filter by content type, monitored status, and premieres/finales</ul>
<ul>Scroll-to-today for easy navigation</ul>
</li>

<b>Activity Queue</b>
<li>
<ul>Real-time download monitoring across all download clients</ul>
<ul>View progress, ETA, and detailed status</ul>
<ul>Manage downloads with options to remove, blocklist, or skip redownload</ul>
</li>

<b>Advanced Features</b>
<li>
<ul><b>Android App Shortcuts:</b> Jump to services or search directly from your home screen</ul>
<ul><b>Custom Headers:</b> Add custom HTTP headers per instance</ul>
<ul><b>Local Network Switching:</b> Automatically use local URLs when connected to home Wi-Fi</ul>
<ul><b>Slow Instance Mode:</b> Configurable timeout for remote or slower instances</ul>
</li>

v0.5.0

[New Features]
• Combined Dashboard: A centralized view for all your Arr instances, download clients, and requests.
• Listenarr Integration: Full support for the Listenarr audiobook management server, including library browsing, searching, and activity tracking.
• Android App Shortcuts: Long-press the app icon to quickly jump to your favorite services or search directly.

[Improvements]
• UI Overhaul: Redesigned media details headers with better logo integration and layout optimization.
• Theming & Polish: Consistent title styling, layout improvements, and haptic feedback throughout the app.
• Performance: Optimized data fetching and caching for multi-instance setups.

[Bug Fixes]
• Fixed navigation loops when opening services from the app launcher.
• Resolved layout issues with overlapping navigation titles and header images.
• Fixed various data synchronization issues when switching between local and remote networks.
